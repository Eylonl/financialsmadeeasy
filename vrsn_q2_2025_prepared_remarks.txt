PREPARED REMARKS FOR EARNINGS CALL
VeriSign Second Quarter 2025 Results

MANAGEMENT COMMENTARY

Chief Executive Officer Opening Remarks - July 25, 2025

Good afternoon and thank you for joining VeriSign's second quarter 2025 earnings call. I'm Jim Bidzos, Executive Chairman, President and CEO of VeriSign, Inc. (NASDAQ: VRSN).

FINANCIAL HIGHLIGHTS:
- Revenue: $387.2 million for Q2 2025
- Net income: $198.5 million for Q2 2025
- Diluted earnings per share: $1.95 for Q2 2025

REVISED BUSINESS OUTLOOK AND GUIDANCE:

For the third quarter of 2025, VeriSign expects:
- Revenue to be in the range of $392 million to $398 million
- Operating margin to be approximately 66%
- Effective tax rate to be in the range of 21% to 23%

For the full year 2025, VeriSign expects:
- Revenue to be in the range of $1.57 billion to $1.61 billion
- Free cash flow to be approximately $875 million
- Capital expenditures to be in the range of $42 million to $47 million

"Following our detailed quarterly review, we are updating our guidance to reflect improved operational efficiency and market conditions," said Jim Bidzos, Executive Chairman, President and Chief Executive Officer.

CONFERENCE CALL INFORMATION:
VeriSign will host a live conference call today at 4:30 p.m. (EDT) to review the revised second quarter 2025 results and updated guidance.
