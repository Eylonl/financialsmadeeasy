VERISIGN, INC.
EARNINGS RELEASE - Q2 2025

FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE

VeriSign Reports Second Quarter 2025 Results

RESTON, VA - July 25, 2025 - VeriSign, Inc. (NASDAQ: VRSN), a global provider of domain name registry services and internet infrastructure, today reported financial results for the second quarter ended June 30, 2025.

FINANCIAL HIGHLIGHTS:
- Revenue: $387.2 million for Q2 2025
- Net income: $198.5 million for Q2 2025
- Diluted earnings per share: $1.95 for Q2 2025

BUSINESS OUTLOOK AND GUIDANCE:

For the third quarter of 2025, VeriSign expects:
- Revenue to be in the range of $390 million to $395 million
- Operating margin to be approximately 65%
- Effective tax rate to be in the range of 22% to 24%

For the full year 2025, VeriSign expects:
- Revenue to be in the range of $1.55 billion to $1.58 billion
- Free cash flow to be approximately $850 million
- Capital expenditures to be in the range of $45 million to $50 million

"We delivered solid results in the second quarter and remain confident in our outlook for the remainder of 2025," said Jim Bidzos, Executive Chairman, President and Chief Executive Officer.
